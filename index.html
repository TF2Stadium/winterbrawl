Why go outside in the cold?

Sure, there are snowballs and sleds and all other manners of merriment out there, but with it comes wet, cold, and misery. Why subject yourself to that when you can sit inside with guns, sharp objects and explosions? TF2Stadium, TF2's opensource and totally free lobby site, is excited to announce the premiere of its first seasonal tournament: The TF2Stadium WinterBrawl! So grab your favorite electric blanket, stoke the fire, gather up 5 of your nerd friends and throw away those scarves and itchy hats; there's cash to be won and dreams to be crushed.

Rules And Regulations

  • The tournament uses the ESEA ruleset, maplist and the Joint Banlist
  • Please follow all of the TF2Stadium rules and code of conduct. Offclassing rules do not apply.
  • Group stages will be a best of 1, round robin style tournament with the top two teams in each group advancing to the brackets
  • Bracket stage best of one, single elimination
  • Prize pool: Guaranteed 300$ for first place, 100$ for second.
  • Pool increases with purchases of server products from our sponsor Tragic Servers when using promo code BRAWL for 35% off the lifetime of any TragicServers.com product.
